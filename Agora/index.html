Agora

Greet. Meet. Repeat.

Agora is a peer to peer app that enables tangible connections and in-person meetups between people in varying circles. Agora offers meetup options complete with venues to users with compatible availability in nearby locations. Agora will match two (or more) users with similar openings in their calendars who are nearby each other for a coffee break, walk, or meal. Agora provides people with a simple process of getting to know someone new.
